DOWNTOWN DUBAI - BLVD HEIGHTS

BLVD Heights epitomises what it means to live, work and thrive in the bustling centre of Dubai. Modelled on typical New York and Chicago these skyscrapers, this 53- and 46-storey twin-tower complex exude luxury, elegance and contemporary chic. The beautifully arranged one-, two-, three- and four-bedroom apartments, penthouses and duplexes are designed with your utmost convenience, comfort and wellbeing in mind.
