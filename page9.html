DOWNTOWN DUBAI - IL PRIMO

The 77-storey Il Primo rises in the heart of The Opera District, only steps away from the iconic Dubai Opera. Its four-, five- and six-bedroom apartments and duplex units range from 484 to 1,059 square metres, occupying half or entire floors. Experience life in spacious, beautifully crafted apartments with an unparalleled breadth of amenities surrounded by spellbinding views and year-round entertainment.
