DUBAI CREEK HARBOUR - HARBOUR VIEWS

The statuesque Harbour Views are the tallest twin towers on Creek Island. Home to more than 750 one-, two- and three-bedroom glass-fronted apartments, the premium residences soar 51 floors above the glistening Dubai Creek. Anchored to a three-level podium comprising additional apartments and 14 exclusive villas overlooking the park, this ultra-modern, multi-functioning structure represents a brand-new vision for urban living.
